Congo-Brazzaville

Congo-Brazzaville, officially known as the Republic of the Congo, is a country located in Central Africa. It is known for its rich biodiversity, with vast rainforests and a variety of wildlife. The capital city is Brazzaville, situated along the Congo River, directly across from Kinshasa, the capital of the Democratic Republic of the Congo.
